Recipe: Taco

Description

The kind of taco i make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beet

  • 1 bag taco seasoning

  • Assorted toppings(diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, shredded lettuce.)

  • Flour tortillas

  • Sour cream

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a pan.

  2. Add seasoning on low heat and stir well, move to bowl and set aside.

  3. Lightly cook the tortillas on the same pan.

  4. Add beef to tortilla and toppings as desired

  5. Add sour cream on top.

  6. Serve and enjoy.
