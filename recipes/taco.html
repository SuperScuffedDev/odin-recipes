Recipe: Taco

Description

The kind of taco i make at home.

Ingredients

    1 lb ground beet

    1 bag taco seasoning

    Assorted toppings(diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, shredded lettuce.)

    Flour tortillas

    Sour cream

Directions

    Step 1

    Brown the ground beef in a pan.

    Step 2

    Add seasoning on low heat and stir well, move to bowl and set aside.

    Step 3

    Lightly cook the tortillas on the same pan.

    Step 4

    Add beef to tortilla and toppings as desired

    Step 5

    Add sour cream on top.

    Step 6

    Serve and enjoy.
