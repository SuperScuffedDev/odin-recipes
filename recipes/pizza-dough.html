Recipe: Pizza Dough

Description

Pizza dough that can be baked with any topping of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • 1 package active dry yeast

  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar

  • 3/4 cup warm water

  • 2 cups bread flour

  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided

Directions

  1. Gather all Ingredients.

  2. Stir yeast, sugar, and warm water together in a small bowl. Let stand until foamy, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir 2 cups of bread flour and salt together in the bowl of a stand mixer. Add yeast mixture and 2 tablespoons of olive oil; beat with dough hook on medium speed until dough is soft and slightly tacky, about 8 minutes, adding up to 3 tablespoons more flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing after each addition.

  4. Cover and let rise at room temperature until doubled in volume, at least 1 hour or up to 2 hours. (Alternatively, cover and let rise in the refrigerator overnight.)

  5. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C) with oven rack positioned in lower third. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface. Shape into a round and roll into a thin crust. Place on a large parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza pan.

  6. Top with desired pizza sauce and toppings. Brush edges with remaining 1 tablespoon oil.

  7. Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden and cheese is melted bubbly, about 12 to 15 minutes.

  8. Serve and enjoy.
